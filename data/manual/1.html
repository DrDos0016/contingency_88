     Dear Mr. Miller,

After sixteen years of service to the good people of Roosylvania. It pains me to see the polls showing a clear loss for reelection this November. After numerous scandals, I fear I have lost all credibility with the voters of this state and will no longer be able to fulfill the wishes of the party.

Most worrying, are the numerous calls for my resignation from the Blues. The more they yell, the more people seem to listen.

The people of this state are ignorant and lazy. They do not understand that our vision is the correct one and are instead actively being deceived by the Blue party.

As my position and the party's power are at stake I am afraid I must request the initiation of Contingency 88.
I respect any concerns you have, but wish to remind you that your employment is riding on this election as much as mine. Please get to work as soon as possible.

Sincerely,
   Governor Anderson
