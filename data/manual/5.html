Controls

The Contingency 88 software relies heavily on the use of a mouse. If you are unfamiliar with this peripheral please delegate use of this software to someone with a background in advanced computing.

Left clicking on a tile will zone it as the selected district or if the tile is already the selected district, unzone it entirely. You may hold the mouse button down to zone multiple tiles with a single button press.

Right clicking on a tile will fill that area with the selected district, or if the tile is already the selected district, unzone the area. This feature is a bit buggy at times. For best results, create an enclosed area and then fill the inside, ideally clicking on a tile just below the top border.

The mouse wheel can be used to cycle through which district you will zone tiles as. If you do not have a mouse with a wheel you can simply press the appropriate number key to select a district

After completing your zoning, simply click the red FINALIZE button in the sidebar to confirm your choice and run the election.

However there are requirements that MUST be met before finalizing. The percent of tiles undistricted must read 0%. Also the difference between the largest district and the smallest district must not exceed 20%. Ignoring either of these will bring too much attention to the districting process and be met with overwhelming hostility.

If you are ever forgetful about how to use this software effectively, hit F1 to open a copy of the manual.

When the time comes to exit the program, on most screens you may simply press Escape. Your current progress in a level is NOT saved. However you will be able to start from that level again in the future.
